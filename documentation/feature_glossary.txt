AVANI AGRO FOODS - HOSTINGER FEATURE GLOSSARY
==========================================================

This glossary explains exactly what the icons in your Hostinger Services menu mean 
for your specific "Avani Agro Foods" project.

--- 1. HOSTING SECTION ---

- WEB HOSTING (The Globe Icon): 
  The "House" for your website. This is the field where you upload your `dist` folder.
  
- NODE.JS WEB APPS (JS Icon):
  A powerful field for running background programs. Not needed for your site today, 
  but used if you ever want to add a real-time database.

- CLOUD HOSTING (Cloud Icon):
  A faster, more expensive version of Web Hosting. Use this if your traffic 
  reaches over 100,000 visitors per month.

--- 2. WEBSITES SECTION ---

- WEBSITE BUILDER (Pencil Icon):
  A tool for beginners. **NOTE**: Do not use this. You already have a professional 
  custom-coded site which is much better than what the builder creates.

- ECOMMERCE WEBSITE BUILDER (Cart Icon):
  A tool for simple online shops. **NOTE**: Avoid this. Your B2B Store is custom-built 
  to handle the unique "Verified Partner" logic you requested.

--- 3. DOMAINS SECTION ---

- DOMAIN NAME SEARCH (Globe with Magnifying Glass):
  The field where you buy your address (e.g., `avaniagrofoods.com`).

- DOMAIN TRANSFER (Arrow Icon):
  Used if you ever want to move your website address away from Hostinger to another provider.

--- 4. EMAIL SECTION ---

- BUSINESS EMAIL (Envelope Icon):
  The field where you create professional addresses like `sales@avaniagrofoods.com`. 
  This increases trust significantly with international B2B partners.

- EMAIL MARKETING (Megaphone Icon):
  Used to send bulk emails (newsletters) to all the Manufacturers and Importers 
  listed in your directories.

--- 5. VPS SECTION ---

- VPS HOSTING:
  "Virtual Private Server". High-level hosting for experts. You don't need this for 
  the next 2 years as your Business Plan is plenty powerful.
